## Private AI chat

Privyy provides end-to-end encrypted infrastructure for LLM chat conversations — process confidential documents, analyze sensitive data, and brainstorm strategies with AI that never trains on your data or exposes your secrets.

Access to a range of open-source models, with encrypted storage and client-side key generation so conversations stay private by construction, not by policy.
