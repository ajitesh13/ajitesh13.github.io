## Audit-grade logging for AI agents

Lookover captures a tamper-evident audit trail for every decision and tool call an AI agent makes — built for the compliance and legal buyers who need to prove *why* an agent did something, not just replay *what* it did.

It intercepts agent actions at the SDK level, checks them against policy (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, EU AI Act framings), and exports signed, structured logs to systems like S3, Splunk, or a SIEM.

## Positioning

Deliberately distinct from developer-facing observability tools like LangSmith or Langfuse — Lookover is built for the compliance and security side of the house, not the debugging workflow.
